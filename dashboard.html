The honest thesis

  • Edge is thin and partly overfit — true of almost any FX/metals momentum game. We don't pretend otherwise.
  • We win on discipline, not a magic signal: survive the knockout (equity carries), contend for the $10k Sharpe prize via a smooth market-neutral book, and take the Technology Prize via the full sponsor integration.
  • Every risk path is bounded — concentration, net-directional, gross/margin, plus a drawdown ladder — so we never touch the forced-liquidation DQ.
  • Negative results kept honest: volatility-target overlay, mv+inverse-vol ensemble, HRP and regime gating were all tested and rejected for underperforming.
Backtest: full month, real per-instrument spreads, hourly rebalance, production risk engine. Past simulated performance is not indicative of live results.
